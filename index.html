Healthy Basics

Sleep

Use a fixed sleep/wake schedule, lower screen use before bed, and keep your room cool and dark.

Food

Build meals with vegetables, protein, whole grains, and fruit. Keep sugary snacks occasional.

Movement

Take short walks, do light strength work, and stretch daily to support mood and energy.

Simple Daily Routine

  • Morning: Hydrate, get sunlight, and do 10 minutes of movement.
  • Afternoon: Keep lunch balanced and take a short walk.
  • Evening: Light dinner, lower caffeine, and reduce screen exposure.
  • Night: Wind down with reading, breathwork, or journaling.

FAQ

How quickly can habits improve energy?

Most people notice small improvements in 1–2 weeks with consistent sleep and activity.

Do I need a strict diet plan?

No. Start with simple, repeatable meal patterns and portion awareness.

What if I miss a day?

Reset the next day. Consistency over time matters more than perfection.
