Myths vs Facts

Masturbation se chehra kala nahi hota. Yeh sirf ek aam galat dharna hai.

Neend ki kami, paani kam peena, junk food, sun damage, stress — yeh hain asli kaaraN. Masturbation ka face colour se koi sambandh nahi.

Bahut log mante hain ki masturbation se body weak ho jaati hai.

Hafte mein 2–4 baar bilkul normal hai. Isse stress kam hota hai, neend achhi aati hai. Kamzori ka kaaraN poor diet aur exercise ki kami hai.

Ek ejaculation mein sirf 0.1% zinc daily requirement ka kharach hota hai. 1 anda khaane se kami poori. Body roz 10–20 crore naye sperm banaati hai.

❌ MYTH: Masturbation se height rukti hai

Yeh bhi ek popular myth hai jo log mante hain.

✅ FACT: Height genetics + nutrition par depend hai

Height Growth hormone (HGH) par depend karti hai jiska masturbation se koi lena dena nahi. Proper sleep aur nutrition se height badhti hai.

❌ MYTH: Memory aur concentration kharab hoti hai

Bahut log kehte hain ki masturbation se dimag kamzor hota hai.

✅ FACT: Guilt aur anxiety se hota hai problem

Masturbation directly brain ko damage nahi karta. Par guilt, shame aur anxiety — yeh mental health ko affect karte hain. Healthy attitude rakhein.

Aksar Pooche Jaane Waale Sawaal

Aapke sabse common questions ke jawab

Hafte mein kitni baar masturbation normal hai? ▼
Hafte mein 2–4 baar bilkul normal aur healthy hai. Koi fixed number nahi hai — har insaan alag hota hai. Jab tak aap physically aur mentally theek feel kar rahe hain, koi baat nahi. Agar roz 3–4+ baar ho raha hai aur aap rok nahi pa rahe, tab doctor se milein.
Kya masturbation se testosterone kam hota hai? ▼
Short-term mein thoda change ho sakta hai, lekin yeh normal range mein rehta hai aur jaldi wapas aa jaata hai. Studies mein paya gaya ki 7 din abstinence ke baad testosterone 145% tak badh sakta hai temporarily. Lekin regular moderate masturbation se long-term testosterone level par koi bura effect nahi hota.
Sex ke baad kitna paani peena chahiye? ▼
Sex ke turant baad 2–3 gilaas paani peena chahiye. Yeh body ko rehydrate karta hai, electrolytes balance karta hai aur UTI (urinary tract infection) se bachata hai. Naariyal paani aur bhi better option hai kyunki ismein natural electrolytes hote hain.
Sperm count badhane ke liye kya karein? ▼
Sperm count badhane ke liye: Zinc se bhare khaane (kaddoo ke beej, ande), Vitamin C (nimbu, amla), Folic acid (palak, methi), Ashwagandha (studies mein 167% sperm count increase), Omega-3 (akhrot, machli), regular exercise, stress management, aur zyaada garam cheezoin se bachein. Smoking aur alcohol sperm count significantly ghata dete hain.
Face glow ke liye sabse zaroori cheez kya hai? ▼
Face glow ke liye andar se bahar — 3 sabse zaroori cheezein hain: (1) Paani — roz 8–10 gilaas, (2) Neend — 7–8 ghante, (3) Vitamin C waale fruits — aamla, nimbu, santra. Bahar se — Aloe vera roz lagao aur sunscreen zaroor use karo. Yeh 3 cheezein 4 hafte mein visible fark laa sakti hain.
Kya Ashwagandha safe hai? Kaise lein? ▼
Haan, Ashwagandha generally safe hai. Dosage: 300–600mg daily, raat ko sone se pehle garam dudh mein. Isse testosterone badhta hai, stress kam hota hai, sperm count aur motility improve hoti hai. Pregnant women, thyroid patients aur kisi bhi serious condition mein pehle doctor se milein. 3 mahine tak regularly lene par best results milte hain.
Masturbation band karne se kya fayda hoga? ▼
Agar aap moderate masturbation (hafte mein 2–3 baar) kar rahe hain toh band karne ki koi zaroorat nahi. Agar aap NoFap ya abstinence try karna chahte hain, toh benefits mein zyaada energy, better focus, aur real relationships mein improvement ho sakti hai. Lekin yeh scientifically proven nahi hai. Main benefit yeh hota hai ki time aur energy productive kamon mein use hoti hai.
